Hello.

I’m a UX/UI Designer finding a new home. With empathy and compassion I help design products and experiences that are truly user-oriented through empowerment, simplicity, and moments of joy.

I studied Media Arts at the University of New Mexico as well as Filmmaking at the Santa Fe University of Art and Design.

I’ve worked for five years as a Set Costumer in both LA and Albuquerque, and I’m a member of IATSE Local 480. Before that, I worked as a UI Designer at Apex Evaluation.

After re-examining my passions and priorities amidst lockdowns, I’ve decided to delve back into the world of design. I recently completed an intensive 10-week UX/UI bootcamp with Ironhack Paris. I’m so excited to be working in a creative field with the ability to truly touch people’s lives through the products they use and experiences they live.

Aside from this, I love taking photos, reading, and drinking cortados. I also really love my cat, Kitten.
